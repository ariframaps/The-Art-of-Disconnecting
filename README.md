# 📴 The Art of Disconnecting — Immersive JOMO Presentation

An award-winning inspired, immersive, and high-end scroll-based presentation website built to unpack the sosiological and cognitive impacts of social media addiction, introducing **JOMO (The Joy of Missing Out)** as the ultimate mental sovereignty.

Designed with a brutalist aesthetic, oversized kinetic typography, fluid scroll-jacking, and a synchronized real-time speaker teleprompter system.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

- **Awwwards-Grade Interaction:** Seamless full-screen scroll snapping using native CSS `snap-y snap-mandatory` mechanics.
- **Synchronized Teleprompter (Dual-Mode UI):**
    - *Left View (2/3):* Ultra-minimalist, typographic slides perfect for the audience.
    - *Right View (1/3):* Real-time speaker notes and prompt scripts that automatically sync based on the active viewport (Desktop only).
- **Kinetic Typography:** Built-in micro-interactions and staggered animation triggers on viewport entry using Framer Motion.
- **Fully Responsive:** Beautifully responsive layouts that hide the technical teleprompter on mobile devices to preserve screen estate.

## 📊 Presentation Architecture & Content Data

The deck follows a strict storytelling progression structural arc: **The Problem → The Zero (Withdrawal) → The Hero (Resolution & CTA)**.

### 1. The Manifesto (Hero)
* **Anchor:** `THE ART OF DISCONNECTING.`
* **Concept:** Reclaiming attention sovereignty in the age of infinite noise.

### 2. The Digital Drift (The Epidemic)
* **Anchor:** `50 DAYS / YEAR.`
* **Data Metrics:** Unveiling that the average Indonesian spends 3.5 hours/day ($\pm$ 50 days a year) scrolling social media.
* *Source:* [DataReportal / We Are Social 2024](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2024-indonesia)

### 3. The Dopamine Engine (The Bio-Hack)
* **Anchor:** `SLOT MACHINE.`
* **Concept:** The psychology of *Variable Reward Systems* utilized by tech algorithms to induce endless feedback loops.
* *Source:* Dr. Anna Lembke, Stanford University (*Dopamine Nation*)

### 4. The Withdrawal (Breaking Code)
* **Anchor:** `PHANTOM VIBRATION.`
* **Concept:** Addressing the biological resistance, anxiety peaks, and hallucinated haptic vibrations upon logging out.
* *Source:* University of Pennsylvania & BBC Tech

### 5. The Cognitive Toll (The Illusion)
* **Anchor:** `ATTENTION RESIDUE.`
* **Concept:** Proving how a single 30-second glance at notifications imposes a 23-minute cognitive recovery penalty.
* *Source:* Dr. Gloria Mark / Harvard Business Review

### 6. The Resolution (Embracing JOMO)
* **Anchor:** `RADICAL PEACE.`
* **Concept:** Mitigating downsides through intentional curation via Google News (Pull vs Push information mechanism) and leveraging ignorance for deeper real-world connection.
* *Source:* Psychology Today

### 7. The Architecture of Contrarian (CTA)
* **Anchor:** `TAKE CONTROL.`
* **Execution:** A three-fold actionable protocol: Amputate non-essential alerts, bury addictive hooks, and host a 24-hour Digital Sabbath.
